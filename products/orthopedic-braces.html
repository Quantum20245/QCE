Orthopedic Braces

Find Relief from Back and Joint Pain — Covered by Medicare!

Relief for Back, Knee, Shoulder, Wrist & Ankle Pain — Covered by Medicare

Back, knee, and joint pain are common and can interfere with your ability to move, sleep, or go about daily activites. Whether caused by injury, surgery, posture, or aging, these issues can reduce your quality of life.

If you're experiencing chronic pain, stiffness, or instability, you may qualify for a Medicare-covered orthopedic brace. These devices are designed to satbilize the affected area, reduce strain, and promote healing — all while supporting your mobility and comfort.

Quantum Care Essentials works with your doctor to coordinate the prescription and approval process, ensuring you revieve your brace with no out-of-pocket cost if eligible through Medicare.

Prescription required. Call us today to check your eligibility and start the process.

Comprehensive Orthopedic Braces

From back and knee support to wrist, shoulder, and ankle stabilization — our braces are built to relieve pain, aid rehabilitation, and restore confidence in movement.

Lumbar Back Brace

Supports relief from:

  • Chronic lower back pain
  • Lumbar instability or disc degeneration
  • Herniated or bulging discs
  • Postural support for spinal alignment
  • Osteoarthritis or spinal stenosis
  • Post-surgical recovery or injury prevention

Shoulder Brace

Designed to assist with:

  • Rotator cuff injuries or tears
  • Shoulder dislocations or instability
  • Post-surgical support
  • Tendonitis or bursitis
  • Range of motion limitations
  • Arthritis-related shoulder pain

Wrist Brace

Supports conditions like:

  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Tendonitis or repetitive strain injuries
  • Wrist sprains and instability
  • Post-fracture immobilization
  • Arthritis of the wrist
  • Post-operative recovery

Knee Brace

Supports recovery from:

  • ACL and MCL injuries
  • Cartilage and meniscus tears
  • Patella and tibia/fibula fractures
  • Hyperextension and ligament derangements
  • Osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis
  • Post-surgery rehab and knee stabilization

Ankle Brace

Helps manage:

  • Ankle sprains or ligament tears
  • Tendon injuries or instability
  • Post-operative or post-cast support
  • Arthritis-related ankle stiffness
  • Chronic swelling or weakness
